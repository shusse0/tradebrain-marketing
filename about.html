OUR STORY

The compliance tools available to most defense exporters were built for Fortune 500 procurement teams — not the people actually doing the work.

Export compliance is one of the highest-stakes workflows in international trade. A single misclassification carries penalties up to $364,000 per violation. An unlicensed export to a sanctioned party can mean criminal liability. Yet for most companies — particularly small and mid-sized defense contractors — the process still runs on spreadsheets, tribal knowledge, and manual cross-referencing across three different government websites.

"We built TradeBrain because compliance teams were spending hours on classification, using patchwork tools, and missing shipment deadlines because of manual screening. We built the platform we wished existed."
— TradeBrain, Defense Trade Solutions

TradeBrain consolidates HTS Import classification, Schedule B Export classification, ECCN licensing analysis, restricted party screening, ITAR form generation, and license lifecycle tracking into one platform. Results that are accurate, defensible, and audit-ready.

OUR PRINCIPLES

Four things we will never compromise on.

01

Regulatory accuracy first

Every classification is built against the actual government data files. HTS, Schedule B, CCL, and Country Chart. No approximations. Results that hold up in a DDTC or BIS audit.

Every feature was built to solve a real compliance problem we encountered in practice. The DSP-5 wizard, license tracker, and ScreenIQ were built because our clients needed them.

03

Defensible outputs

Every result includes a full audit trail with correction chains. Every classification includes the reasoning behind it. Built to satisfy a federal auditor, not just a compliance checkbox.

04

SMB-first pricing
