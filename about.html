What TradeBrain is

TradeBrain is a single platform that consolidates every core trade compliance workflow into one place. Instead of juggling separate tools for HTS and Schedule B classification, denied party screening, export licensing, ITAR documentation, and audit reporting, compliance teams get a unified system that handles it all. Product descriptions go in; validated classification codes, screening results, license determinations, and audit-ready documentation come out. The result is faster decisions, fewer errors, and a complete compliance record your team can trust during audits.
